HSR SafetyBase.

SafetyBase is a web-based data tool for Health and Safety Representatives to create and keep safety records. SafetyBase includes: pre-start checklists; training records; incident reports and; electrical test and tag register.

SafetyBase is designed for structural trade contractors in the construction industry.
